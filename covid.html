About Covid-19

What is Covid-19 (corona-virus)

covid-19 is the infection disease coused by the most recently discovered coronavirus. This new virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak in china, wuhan, in december 2019.

Coronavirus are a large family of viruses which may cause illness in animals or human. In human, several cronavirus are known to cause respiratory infections ranging from common cold to more severe disease such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome(MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome(SARS). The most recently discovered coronavirus causes coronavirus disease COVID-19.

Coronavirus Symptoms

Steps of Prevention Against Coronavirus

Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds, wih soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub.

Stay home and self isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell.

Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when you cough or sneeze.

If you have fever, cough and diffculty breathin, seek medical care earily.

Avoid close contact (1meter or 3 feet) with people are unwell.

Stay informed by watching news and follow the recommended practice.
